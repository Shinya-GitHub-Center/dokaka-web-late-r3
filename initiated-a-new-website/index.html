I just have deployed my new website. I'm gonna attach some sound clips regularly to this blog. You can identify each song by the tag attribution.

  • OldSong : Around 2004, very old material
  • RecentSong : From 2008 to 2019, relatively recent track
  • LatestSong : After 2020 and the latest material
  • Original : Original Song
  • Cover : Cover Song
  • MemoTrack : Simple track, made for an idea recording or for fun
  • IsolTrack : Isolated track before mixdown
  • RoughTrack : Rough sketch, semi multi-track material
  • FullTrack : Seriously crafted multi-track material
  • Released : Already published or disclosed through CD, web, radio, etc...
  • Unreleased : 100% undisclosed material until now
  • Discontinued : Discontinued material
  • Information : News or latest information from Dokaka.com
  • Blog : My rustic diary, daily Blog

Please enjoy an old school-oriented website, the flavor which the modern SNS technology can not showcase.
